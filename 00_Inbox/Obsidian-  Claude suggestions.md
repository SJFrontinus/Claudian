**My Recommendation for Your Daily Notes**

  

  **Try this simple approach first:**

  

  1. **Install: Tasks plugin**

    - Easiest to start

    - Tracks your checkbox items

    - No external account needed

  2. **Then optionally add: RemindMeWhen**

    - Reminds you of unchecked tasks

    - Perfect for task propagation

  

  ---

  **Step-by-Step: Installing Tasks Plugin**

  

  1. Open Obsidian Settings (gear icon)

  2. Click "Community plugins" in left sidebar

  3. Click "Browse"

  4. Search box: type Tasks

  5. Click the "Tasks" result by Matt Pocock

  6. Click "Install"

  7. Click "Enable" (toggle turns green)

  8. Close settings

  

  **Now you can use it in your daily notes:**

  ## Tasks for Today

  - [ ] Review research

  - [ ] Write one idea

  - [ ] Update book club list

  

  The Tasks plugin will track these with features like:

  - Due dates

  - Recurring tasks

  - Task filtering

  - Sorting by priority

  

  ---

  **Want to Try Now?**

  

  Open Obsidian, go to Settings → Community plugins → Browse, search for Tasks, and install it. Takes 30 seconds.
