# Book Club

Your space for tracking and exploring non-fiction titles from book club.

## Folders

- **Reading List**: Titles to read or being discussed
- **Authors**: One page per author - track their other works
- **Meeting Notes**: Discussion notes and reflections from meetings

## How to Use

1. Add books to Reading List as they're selected
2. Create author pages when you discover interesting authors
3. Link authors to their books
4. Use Meeting Notes to capture discussion insights
5. Cross-reference with your Research areas (AI, Brain Health, etc.)

## Research Connections

Many book club selections will connect to your research interests. Look for links to:
- AI and cognition
- Brain health and neuroscience
- Medical/pharmacological insights
- Methodology and thinking systems
